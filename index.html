Our Story

A cup steeped in tradition

At Tea Toria, every cup begins with a flame and ends with a smile. We revive the ancient art of tandoor chai — bold, smoky, and brewed the way it was meant to be.

Located near the serene Kaliasot Dam in Bhopal, Tea Toria is where chai lovers come for flavour and stay for the vibes. Our handcrafted kulhad chai, lovingly prepared with old‑world techniques, is what sets us apart.
