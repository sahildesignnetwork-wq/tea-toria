Our Story

Born from a love for chai

Tea Toria started with a simple idea — what if chai could be more than just a drink? What if it could be an experience?

In 2024, near the serene Kaliasot Dam in Bhopal and now serving Delhi NCR, we brought that idea to life. We revived the ancient art of tandoor chai — using a real clay tandoor to give our chai a bold, smoky depth that no ordinary cup can match.

Today, Tea Toria is Delhi NCR and Bhopal's favourite chai spot — a place where students, families, and friends gather every evening to enjoy incredible chai, delicious food, and the most amazing sunset views.

"We don't just serve chai. We serve tradition, community, and memories — one kulhad at a time."

The Visionary Behind

Meet the founder

Tea Toria is the dream of Sarvesh — a passionate chai lover who believed that a simple cup of chai could become an unforgettable experience. From sourcing the finest Assam leaves to perfecting the tandoor firing technique — every detail is crafted with love and precision.

What started as a small idea near Kaliasot Dam has grown into Delhi NCR and Bhopal's most loved chai destination, thanks to Sarvesh's relentless dedication to quality, community, and the art of tandoor chai.
